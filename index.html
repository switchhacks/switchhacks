switch:hacks

The geniuses over at ReSwitched have developed homebrew for Nintendo's newest console Nintendo Switch! The new homebrew environment is called PegaSwitch! It can be installed on firmware version 3.0.0 and NOTHING ELSE. Nothing higher, and nothing lower! In order to get firmware 3.0.0 you have to get a Switch on a firmware lower than 3.0.0. You then have to disconnect from Wi-Fi and use a physical copy of "Pokken Tournament DX" and update with the cartridge.

Installation (3.0.0 only)
